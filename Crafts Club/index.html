Welcome to the Bo'ness Craft Club

We are a group of local craft lovers who get together at the local Cafe every Sunday morning for a cup of tea, a bit of chit-chat and of course crafts! We welcome anyone who wishes to join us to come along.

Please note, that the space is currently limited and we only welcome small crafts, which don't take up too much space and are easy to move around. We do not currently accept any noisy machinery to be brought to the meetings.
