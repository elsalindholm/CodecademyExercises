Services We Offer

Daily walkies

We pick up your dog arround midday and take them for a long walk 1.5-2h along one of our normal routes. Your dog will not only get to go out but also to socialise and play with other dogs.

£12 / day

Daycare

We can take care of your dog for the whole day depending on your needs. Your dog can spend their day at our home with our two dogs. We will take them for many walks depending on the length of their stay.

£15 - £20 per day

Overnight Stays

If you need to go away for a few days, you can leave your dog(s) with us. We will walk your dog three times a day, plus they have access to our garden throghout the day. We will feed them and have a safe space for them to relax and socialise with others.

£25 per night
